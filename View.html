Vehicle History

This numbers-matching 1965 Ford Mustang Fastback has been meticulously restored to original specifications with only minor upgrades for drivability. The car was originally sold in California and spent most of its life in the dry southwest before being acquired by our collection in 2018.

The restoration was completed in 2020 with attention to preserving as many original components as possible. The 289ci V8 engine was rebuilt to factory specifications and mated to the original 4-speed manual transmission. All documentation including original build sheet, owner's manual, and restoration receipts are included.
